About Me

I’m Rushikesh Thanekar, a dedicated GIS Analyst and Geospatial Developer with a strong background in spatial data processing, web GIS development, and geospatial automation. My work spans diverse domains such as urban planning, hydrological modeling, BIM-GIS integration, and 3D visualization. I have hands-on experience with tools and technologies like ArcGIS Pro, ArcMap, QGIS, Python, and CesiumJS.

I specialize in bridging the gap between traditional GIS workflows and modern web technologies. From transforming CAD/BIM data into geospatial formats to building automated pipelines and interactive dashboards, I strive to create solutions that are both technically sound and user-friendly.

Currently, I’m exploring advanced integrations of LiDAR, BIM, and GIS to build immersive digital experiences and smart infrastructure tools. I’m always open to collaboration, innovation, and contributing to meaningful geospatial projects that solve real-world challenges.
